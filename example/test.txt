Stripped Sparrow[wiki] 
The striped sparrow [oriturus.] is a species of bird in the family [pass.]. It is monotypic within the genus Oriturus.

[fig.sparrow]. Striped sparrow
It is endemic to Mexico where its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist montane forest and temperate grassland[IUCN,wiki].
